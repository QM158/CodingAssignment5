Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Whisk cream and sugar in a chilled bowl.
  2. Add vanilla and blend until thickened.
  3. Freeze for 2 hours before serving.

Tips

Try adding fresh fruit or drizzle with chocolate syrup for extra flair!
